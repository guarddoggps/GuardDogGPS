Why should I buy a Guard Dog GPS?
Guard Dog GPS is a unique company. We have been designing, manufacturing and programming our line of GPS devices for cars, vehicle fleets, motorcycles and marine environments for nearly 40 years. Our components come from top multi-national companies, so you can always rely on Guard Dog GPS products.

 

Where can I buy a Guard Dog GPS product?.
Our online store is open 24/7. To order AutoTrac, FleetTrac, MotoTrac or MarineTrac devices. Go to www.guarddoggps.com to place your order.

 

I have an alarm system sign outside my home to deter thieves. Why isn’t there the same information on m y Guard Dog GPS device?
Experience shows tracking devices themselves are not good theft deterrents.  Their value is in fast tracking and recovery of your car by police, before the vehicle is stripped of parts or shipped out of the country.


Is one tracker usable for all types of vehicles?
A: We design and manufacture proprietary Guard Dog GPS devices specifically for cars, vehicle fleets, motorcycles and marine environments.  

Wåhat is bread crumbing?
Our devices provide daily, weekly, monthly and quarterly detailed vehicle travel histories for you to analyze later. Reports show routes, speeds, stops with addresses, whether the engine was left running and for how long, mileage and other information. The data will help you save fuel and maintenance costs, increase productivity, improve safety and provide insights into field activity that can improve overall efficiency.  

Can I get a discount from my insurance company if I have a Guard Dog GPS in my vehicle?
Yes, insurance companies see the value in recovering a stolen vehicle quickly and undamaged. They are willing to encourage their customers to install an engine kill switch, a Guard Dog option, as an incentive for a discount of 10 to 25 percent.  

Am I invading my child’s privacy by tracking her driving?
Rewarding good driving habits in teens and young adults is positive reinforcement. A teenager is more likely to die in an automobile-related accident than an adult, and a feature like a panic alarm is indispensible for personal security.  

How often does the software need to be updated?
Guard Dog GPS programmers are continually writing new code and sending free updates over the air, so you can be assured you will always have the latest features. 

What happens if my vehicle is stolen and hidden in a building or blocked, so that the clear sky signal is degraded?
The signal is automatically switched to cell phone triangulation for continuous tracking.

What is a virtual fence?
You can set an area as small as half a city block or as large as an entire state or larger where you want the vehicle to operate. As soon as the vehicle goes outside that boundary, you receive an immediate alert sent to your hand-held device or computer. 

Does Guard Dog GPS come with a guarantee?

Is there a panic button?
Yes, safety and security is our main concern. All Guard Dog GPS devices have an optional panic button and alert that can be installed.  

How does the optional panic button work?
With the optional panic button installed an alert can be sent if you feel your security is being threatened. A standard feature of all Guard Dog GPS devices is an alert that is sent if the vehicle is involved in an accident or there is an emergency. 

Will I save money using a Guard Dog GPS?
