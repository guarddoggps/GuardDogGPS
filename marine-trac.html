MarineTrac® GPS Tracking device

Guard Dog MarineTrac® is a multi-functional tracking and security system. We proudly design, manufacture and program our devices in the U.S. No lower-cost marine tracking GPS can be found on the market today. Our annual, money-saving airtime plan, with unlimited phone and Web use is the best in the industry. You can select a monthly airtime plan with the same features and benefits. Online activation is quick and easy. There are no contracts or fees for early cancellation.

A variety of features protects crafts of all types, whether it is on land or in the water. MarineTrac was designed by experienced sailors who added additional, practical features. Although the software offers a full suite of functions, MarineTrac is easy to use. Guard Dog MarineTrac safety and security features include a dock/storage virtual fence to alert you if the craft is being moved without authorization. Position, speed and direction are just a few of the robust tracking functions reported in real time to handheld devices or computers. The information can be given to police for swift recovery of your craft.

A waterproof case prevents water from entering the device and damaging the components. Parents can reward good seamanship, while bad piloting can be corrected immediately or the subject of a later discussion. Use it from your handheld device or computer to receive reports and data and set functions.
